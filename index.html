Worker Safety

At Odhav Enterprise, we have developed a culture that promotes an injury-free environment and provides the safest workplace possible for our employees, subcontractors, clients and others who enter or who near our construction sites.

Our Mantra Is: “Every Worker Goes Home From Each Of Our Jobs, Every Day.”

Responsible for delivering Safety requirements at the project site, conducting Safety training covering the employees and contractors at the project site including: Basic Safety Training, Safety Awareness Training, Waste & Chemical Management, and other mandatory HSE training as part of the Client employee induction programs to increase safety awareness and to drive continuous improvements in the safety culture.

Support the delivery of the Safety business plan for the project by acting as Single Point Safety Contact for the project allocated, support the organization in achieving Safety targets.

Sustainability internal audit plan for the project, ensure that the Legal register is reviewed and key measures are monitored relevant to the project. Respect Client’ Policies, principles and Values (act as role model) & Prepare and submit the weekly Safety reports for the site.

Conduct regular system review and enable system improvement by supporting the project team valuate the activities for the week and provide areas of focus to the project team. Develop the HSE plan and Emergency Plan for the project site in line with client’s Safety standards and expectation.
